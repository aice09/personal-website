## Hobby: Homelab

Outside of work, I run a homelab where I get to break things on my own terms instead of in production. It's where I test out ideas before they ever touch anything that matters — new Kubernetes configs, storage setups, networking experiments, or just poking at tools I'm curious about.

It's also just genuinely fun. There's something satisfying about building your own little infrastructure playground, tearing it down, and rebuilding it better the second time around.
